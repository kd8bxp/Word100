Chip 2 also contains 8 different Alphabets (A to Z), English Female, English Male, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, and Spanish.
Since all the Alphabets are the same A to Z (_A to _Z), additional library files have been created.
#include <BBTEnglishF.h> for English Female
#include <BBTEnglishM.h> for English male
#include <BBTFrench.h> for French
#include <BBTGerman.h> for German
#include <BBTItalian.h> for Italian
#include <BBTPortuguese.h> for Portuguese
#include <BBTRussian.h> for Russian
#include <BBTSpanish.h> for Spanish
Of course, all sets of Alphabets can be used, you just need to know it's location, and what chip it is on (Chip Select 2) See Example code for more information.
